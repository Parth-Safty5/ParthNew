"Report on School Annual Function"

This report captures the highlights of the school’s annual function, including performances, awards, and celebrations.

Report Type 1: Formal School Report

Date: 2nd March 2026
Prepared by: Class Teacher, Sunshine High School

Introduction:
The annual function of Sunshine High School was celebrated on 28th February 2026. It was a day of joy, learning, and showcasing student talents. Parents, teachers, and students attended the event enthusiastically.

Events:

  1. Opening Ceremony: The principal inaugurated the function with a motivational speech highlighting the achievements of the school year.

  2. Cultural Performances: Students performed various dances, skits, and songs, reflecting both traditional and modern themes.

  3. Awards and Recognition: Certificates were distributed to students excelling in academics, sports, and arts.

  4. Special Guest Speech: A renowned educationist addressed the students, encouraging them to pursue excellence.

Conclusion:
The function was well-organized and successful, creating a memorable experience for everyone. It not only celebrated achievements but also motivated students to continue striving for excellence.

Report Type 2: Informal Student-Friendly Report

Date: 2nd March 2026
Submitted by: Student Council, Sunshine High School

Introduction:
Our school’s annual function on 28th February 2026 was an amazing celebration! Students and teachers worked hard to make it a fun and inspiring day.

Highlights:

  • Welcome Speech: The principal’s speech made everyone feel proud of our school.

  • Exciting Performances: Dance performances, a short drama, and a choir performance entertained all attendees.

  • Achievements Celebrated: Students who excelled in sports, science projects, and art were awarded certificates and trophies.

  • Fun Activities: A small talent show added a playful touch to the event, making students happy and confident.

Conclusion:
The school annual function was a blend of fun, learning, and celebration. It gave students a platform to show their talents and brought the entire school community together.
