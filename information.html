The Diverse Modes of Transport

Transport is an essential part of our daily lives, connecting people and goods across the world. It can be broadly divided into three main modes: Land, Water, and Air transport. Land transport is the most common, consisting of roadways for shorter distances and railways for longer journeys. Water transport, using ships and boats, is vital for international trade. Finally, air transport via aeroplanes and helicopters is the fastest way to travel globally. Each mode has its own significance in human development.
