Information Transfer

Master Section VI of the SSC English Exam. Convert complex data into simple words or diagrams.

A1. Non-Verbal to Verbal (Diagram to Paragraph)

SSC Marking Scheme (5 Marks): Title (1M) | Use of all points (2M) | Grammar (1M) | Vocabulary & Sentence Construction (1M).

Q: Write a paragraph based on the following Tree Diagram.

Means of Transport
Land: Roadways, Railways
Water: Ships, Boats
Air: Aeroplanes, Helicopters

The Different Modes of Transport

Transport plays a pivotal role in modern civilization by facilitating the movement of people and goods. As shown in the tree diagram, transportation is categorized into three primary modes: Land, Water, and Air. Land transport is widely used and includes roadways for local commuting and railways for heavy cargo or long-distance travel. Water transport remains the backbone of international trade, utilizing ships and boats. Lastly, Air transport, featuring aeroplanes and helicopters, is the fastest mode, connecting the world in hours. Each mode serves a unique purpose in global connectivity.
