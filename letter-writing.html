New Formal Letter Layout (Block Format)

1

Sender's Address & Date

Flush to the left margin. Avoid punctuation at the end of lines.

2

Receiver's Designation & Address

e.g., To, The Editor/The Commissioner.

3

Subject Line & Salutation

Short subject (max 1 line) followed by "Respected Sir/Madam".

4

Body & Subscription

3 Paragraphs (Intro, Detail, Request) then "Yours faithfully".

Informal Letter Format (Personal)

1

Personal Address & Date

Start from the top left corner.

2

Friendly Salutation

Dear [Name], My dear Father/Mother.

3

The Main Body

Casual and warm tone. Ask about well-being and share your news.

4

Loving Subscription

Yours lovingly / Your loving son/daughter.

Formal Letter: Garbage Complaint

×
A-204, Surya Residency, Gokhale Road, Pune - 411002. 24th February, 2026. To, The Sanitary Officer, Municipal Corporation, Pune. Subject: Complaint regarding irregular clearance of garbage. Respected Sir, I am writing this letter to bring to your kind attention the miserable condition of our locality due to the irregular clearance of garbage. For the past one week, the garbage collection van has not visited our area. As a result, heaps of stinking garbage are lying open on the roads. This has become a breeding ground for mosquitoes and flies, posing a serious threat to the health of the residents. We fear an outbreak of diseases like malaria or dengue if immediate action is not taken. I earnestly request you to look into this matter personally and ensure that the garbage is cleared daily. We hope for a quick response from your side. Yours faithfully, Parth New

Informal Letter: Invitation

×
Plot No. 45, Shanti Nagar, M.G. Road, Mumbai - 400001. 24th February, 2026. Dear Rohan, I hope you and your family are doing great. I am writing this letter with a lot of excitement to invite you to my 15th birthday party on the 2nd of March. As you know, every year we have a small celebration, but this time it's special since it's our last school year. I have organized a small party at my home in the evening. There will be music, some fun games, and of course, a delicious dinner. Most of our old school friends are coming, and the party would be incomplete without your presence. The party starts at 6:30 PM. Please try to come a bit early so we can spend some more time together. Convey my regards to your parents and love to little Tina. I am eagerly waiting for you! Your loving friend, Parth New
