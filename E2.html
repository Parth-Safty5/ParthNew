1.2 The Fun They Had

Warming Up!
Chit-Chat
• What do you like best about your school?
Idea: I love playing with my friends during recess and talking to my teachers in person.

• Do you think school is fun?
Idea: Yes, because I get to learn new things every day and enjoy activities like art or sports with my classmates.

• Which is the part that you don't like?
Idea: I don't like having to wake up very early in the morning, or sometimes I find homework to be boring.

• How often should the teacher give you a test?
Idea: I think a test once a month is good so that we have enough time to learn and understand the lessons before being graded.

2. The following sentences have an unusual meaning in the context of the story. Write what these sentences would mean today, and what they mean in the context of the story.
Sentence Meaning Today Meaning in the Story
(a) She had been hoping they would take the teacher away altogether. She hoped the school would close or the teacher would quit. She hoped they would take the mechanical teacher away for repairs and not bring it back.
(b) They had taken Tommy's teacher away for nearly a month. His teacher was on leave for a month. The mechanical teacher was removed for repairs for a month.
(c) It wasn't a regular teacher. It was a man. A human teacher taught them. The teacher was not a machine, but a human.
(d) How could a man be a teacher? Teachers are usually humans. Since they only know mechanical teachers, they find it impossible for a human to teach.
(e) A teacher has to be adjusted to fit the mind of each boy and girl it teaches. Teachers adapt their teaching style to different students. The mechanical teacher's settings must be changed to match the speed and learning capacity of each child.
