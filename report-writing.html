Republic Day Celebrated with Zeal at New Era School

By Staff Reporter | Pune, Jan 27, 2026

PUNE: New Era High School celebrated India's 77th Republic Day with immense patriotic fervor and enthusiasm on January 26. The entire campus was elegantly decorated in the colors of the national tricolor.


The ceremony commenced at 8:00 AM with the hoisting of the National Flag by the Chief Guest, Retd. Colonel S.P. Deshmukh. As the tricolor unfurled, the atmosphere was filled with the sounds of the National Anthem. This was followed by a disciplined march past by the school's NCC unit and Scout-Guide batches.


In a stirring speech, the Chief Guest highlighted the sacrifices of our martyrs and urged students to uphold the values of the Indian Constitution. Students from Class 10 performed a medley of patriotic songs and a traditional folk dance, which captivated the audience.


The event concluded with the distribution of sweets. The Principal expressed gratitude to all attendees for making the celebration a grand success. The day served as a reminder of our responsibilities towards the nation.
