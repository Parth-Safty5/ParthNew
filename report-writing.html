The 4-Step Format

  • Headline: Catchy and bold title.
  • Dateline: Place, Date and Source (By Staff Reporter).
  • Lead Para: What, when, where, and who.
  • Detail Para: How the incident happened.

Republic Day Celebrated with Zeal at New Era School
By Staff Reporter | Pune, Jan 27:

New Era High School, Pune, celebrated India's 77th Republic Day with great patriotic fervor and enthusiasm on January 26, 2026. The entire school campus was decorated with saffron, white, and green balloons and ribbons.


The event began at 8:00 AM with the hoisting of the National Flag by the Chief Guest, Retd. Colonel S.P. Deshmukh. As the tricolor unfurled, everyone sang the National Anthem with pride. This was followed by a spectacular march past by the school's NCC unit.


In his speech, the Chief Guest reminded the students about the sacrifices made by our freedom fighters and the importance of the Indian Constitution. The cultural program included patriotic songs and a traditional dance performance by the Class 10 students.


The program concluded with the distribution of sweets among students. The Principal thanked the guests and students for making the event a grand success. It was truly a day to remember and cherish.
