Dialogue Writing: Strategy & Examples

Marking Scheme (5 Marks):
  • (a) Jumbled Sentences - 1 Mark
  • (b) Complete the dialogue - 1 Mark
  • (c) Write a dialogue on given theme - 3 Marks

Solved Sample: Discussion on Mobile Addiction


Sneha: Hi Rahul, I noticed you're always on your phone during recess. Are you okay?

Rahul: Yeah, Sneha. I just can't stop playing these games. They are so fun!

Sneha: But don't you think it's affecting your studies and eyesight?

Rahul: I do feel tired and my grades are dropping, but it's hard to quit.

Sneha: You should set a time limit. Try spending more time on the playground instead.
