Dialogue & Speech Writing Guide

Expert-approved English Writing Skills for Maharashtra State Board (SSC) 2026 Exams.

Dialogue Writing Format & Solved Example

SSC Marking Scheme: Prepare dialogue (1M) + Complete dialogue (1M) + Theme-based Dialogue (3M). Total: 5 Marks.

Topic: Discussion on Mobile Addiction between two friends


Sneha: Hi Rahul, I noticed you're always on your phone during recess. Don't you want to join us for football?

Rahul: Hi Sneha. Honestly, I've become so addicted to this new game that I can't keep it down.

Sneha: But don't you think it's affecting your studies and eyesight? You look quite tired lately.

Rahul: You're right. My concentration is decreasing and I often stay up late playing, which makes me sleepy in class.

Sneha: It's time to set a limit, Rahul. Try using an app timer and spend more time outdoors for a fresh mind.

Rahul: That’s a great suggestion. I will start by limiting my gaming to only 30 minutes a day from tomorrow.

Speech Writing Format & Solved Example

SSC Marking Scheme: Greeting (1M) + Body/Content (2M) + Logical Flow (1M) + Conclusion (1M). Total: 5 Marks.

Topic: Importance of Yoga in Daily Life


Honorable Chief Guest, Respected Principal, Teachers, and my dear friends,


A very happy morning to everyone. Today, I am here to speak on a topic that is essential for every student: "Yoga: A Path to a Healthy Life".


In our modern, fast-paced life, we often face mental stress and physical fatigue. Yoga is not just about twisting our bodies; it is a gift from our ancient culture that connects the mind and soul. For students, practicing Yoga daily can significantly improve memory power and concentration.


As the saying goes, "Health is wealth." By dedicating just 20 minutes to Pranayama and Surya Namaskar, we can boost our immunity and stay energetic throughout the day. Let us all pledge to make Yoga an inseparable part of our lives. Thank you!
