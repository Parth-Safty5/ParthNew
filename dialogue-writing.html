Dialogue Writing Format & Example

Marks Distribution: Prepare dialogue (1M) + Complete dialogue (1M) + Theme Dialogue (3M).

Topic: Discussion on Mobile Addiction


Sneha: Hi Rahul, I noticed you're always on your phone during recess.

Rahul: Yeah, Sneha. I just can't stop playing these games. They are so fun!

Sneha: But don't you think it's affecting your studies and eyesight?

Rahul: I do feel tired and my grades are dropping, but it's hard to quit.

Sneha: You should set a time limit. Try spending more time on the playground instead.

Rahul: That’s a good suggestion. I will try to use my phone for only one hour a day from now on.

Speech Writing Format & Tips

Marks Distribution: Greeting (1M) + Body Content (2M) + Logical Flow (1M) + Conclusion (1M).

Topic: Importance of Yoga in Daily Life


Honorable Chief Guest, Principal, and my dear friends,

A very happy morning to everyone. Today, I am going to speak on "Yoga: A Path to Healthy Life".


In today’s stressful world, Yoga is a gift for mankind. It is not just about physical exercise but about connecting the mind and soul. Regular practice of Yoga improves concentration and reduces anxiety, especially for students like us.


As the saying goes, "Health is the greatest wealth." By practicing Surya Namaskar and Pranayama for just 20 minutes daily, we can stay fit and energetic throughout the day.


Let us all pledge to make Yoga a part of our daily routine. Thank you for your time!
