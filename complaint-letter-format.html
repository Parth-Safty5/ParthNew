Complaint Letter Format

Professionally written complaint letters for school, office, and personal concerns.

Complaint Letter 1 – For School Issue

To
The Principal
[School Name]
[City]

Subject: Complaint Regarding [Issue]

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am writing to formally bring to your attention an issue regarding [describe issue, e.g., misbehavior by a fellow student, poor facilities, or academic concerns]. Despite previous attempts to address this matter informally, the problem persists and is affecting my [studies/well-being].

I kindly request your immediate intervention to resolve this issue at the earliest possible time. I trust that appropriate action will be taken to prevent similar occurrences in the future.

Thanking you for your attention to this matter.

Yours sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Class & Section]
[Roll Number]

Complaint Letter 2 – For Office/Workplace Issue

To
The Manager/HR Department
[Company Name]
[City]

Subject: Formal Complaint Regarding [Issue]

Dear [Manager/HR Name],

I am writing to formally lodge a complaint regarding [describe issue, e.g., workplace harassment, technical issues, or delay in payments]. This matter has been affecting my ability to perform my duties efficiently and has caused considerable inconvenience.

I request that a thorough investigation be conducted and corrective measures be taken as soon as possible. I hope my concern will be addressed promptly and appropriately to maintain a professional and safe working environment.

Thanking you in advance for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Designation]
[Department]

Complaint Letter 3 – For Personal/Service Issue

To
The Customer Service Manager
[Organization/Company Name]
[City]

Subject: Complaint Regarding [Product/Service Issue]

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with [describe product or service issue, e.g., delayed delivery, defective product, or poor service]. Despite following all necessary procedures and contacting your support team, the issue remains unresolved, causing inconvenience.

I request that immediate steps be taken to rectify this problem and provide a resolution at the earliest. I trust that my complaint will be treated seriously and that measures will be implemented to prevent recurrence.

Thanking you for your understanding and cooperation.

Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]
[Customer ID if applicable]
[Contact Information]
