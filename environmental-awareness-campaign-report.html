“Environmental Awareness Campaign Report”

This report details the activities and impact of the school’s environmental awareness campaign.

Report Type 1: Formal School Report

Date: 5th March 2026
Prepared by: Science Department, Green Valley School

Introduction:
Green Valley School organized an Environmental Awareness Campaign on 3rd March 2026 to educate students and the community about sustainable practices and the importance of protecting the environment.

Activities Conducted:

  1. Tree Plantation Drive: Students planted over 50 saplings in the school garden and surrounding areas.

  2. Awareness Sessions: Teachers conducted sessions on recycling, water conservation, and reducing plastic usage.

  3. Poster Competition: Students created informative posters to encourage eco-friendly habits.

  4. Community Outreach: Campaign messages were shared on social media and local community boards to reach a wider audience.

Conclusion:
The campaign was successful in spreading awareness and motivating students to take active steps toward environmental protection. The school plans to continue such initiatives in the future to promote sustainability.


Report Type 2: Informal Student-Friendly Report

Date: 5th March 2026
Submitted by: Eco Club, Green Valley School

Introduction:
Our school held an exciting Environmental Awareness Campaign on 3rd March 2026. The goal was to learn how to care for the environment and involve the whole school in eco-friendly activities.

Highlights of the Campaign:

  • Tree Planting: Students planted many trees, making our school greener and cleaner.

  • Fun Learning Sessions: Teachers taught us about recycling, saving water, and avoiding single-use plastics.

  • Creative Posters: Students made colorful posters showing ways to protect nature.

  • Community Sharing: We shared tips and photos online to inspire others to join the green movement.

Conclusion:
The campaign was a fun and educational experience. Everyone enjoyed participating while learning how to make a difference for the planet. It motivated students to adopt eco-friendly habits in daily life.
