Pollution: A Global Threat

Pollution is the introduction of harmful materials into the environment. These harmful materials are called pollutants. Today, pollution has become a major global challenge, affecting the health of the planet and all living beings. It exists in various forms, primarily air, water, soil, and noise pollution.


Air pollution is caused by the smoke released from factories and vehicles, leading to respiratory diseases and global warming. Similarly, water pollution occurs when industrial waste and plastics are dumped into rivers and oceans, killing aquatic life. Soil pollution, caused by excessive use of chemicals in farming, makes the land infertile. The constant noise from traffic and machines also contributes to mental stress and hearing problems.


To save our Earth, we must take immediate action. We should plant more trees, reduce the use of plastic, and promote renewable energy like solar and wind power. As citizens, it is our duty to keep our surroundings clean. If we do not act now, the future of the coming generations will be in danger. Let's pledge to protect nature for a better tomorrow.

My Hobby: Reading Books

A hobby is an activity that we do in our spare time for pleasure and relaxation. It keeps us away from the monotony of daily life. Different people have different hobbies, but my favorite hobby is reading books. To me, books are the windows to the world and my most faithful friends.


Reading books has many benefits. It improves my vocabulary, enhances my imagination, and provides a wealth of knowledge. Whether it is a storybook, a biography, or a science journal, every book has something unique to offer. When I read, I feel like I am traveling to different places and meeting new people without even leaving my room.


My personal library has a collection of over fifty books. I especially enjoy reading about history and space. This hobby has helped me improve my concentration and has made me more creative. As someone once said, "Books are a uniquely portable magic." I truly believe that reading is the best way to utilize time productively and grow as a person.
