1. Write down the relation between the characters given below:
(a) Prospero and Miranda – Father and daughter
(b) Prospero and Antonio – Brothers; Antonio is Prospero’s younger brother who betrayed him
(c) Gonzalo and Prospero – Gonzalo is a loyal courtier who helped Prospero escape after being overthrown
(d) Sycorax and Caliban – Sycorax is Caliban’s mother
(e) Alonso and Sebastian – Brothers; Alonso is the King of Naples and Sebastian is his younger brother
(f) Alonso and Ferdinand – Father and son
2. Find two supporting points from the story to prove the following facts:
(1) Antonio was a disloyal and dangerous man.
(a) He betrayed his brother Prospero and took over the Dukedom of Milan.
(b) He conspired with Sebastian to kill King Alonso during the voyage.

(2) Gonzalo was loyal to the rightful Duke.
(a) He helped Prospero and Miranda escape safely by providing food, clothes, and books.
(b) He remained faithful to Prospero and hoped for justice to be restored.

(3) Prospero was a great magician.
(a) He used his magical powers to create a storm and bring his enemies to the island.
(b) He controlled spirits like Ariel and performed powerful enchantments.

(4) Miranda was very kind hearted
(a) She felt pity for the people caught in the storm and pleaded with her father to stop it.
(b) She showed love and compassion towards Ferdinand and helped him.

(5) Ariel was very faithful to Prospero
(a) Ariel obeyed all of Prospero’s commands without question.
(b) He reminded Prospero of his promise to set him free and continued to serve loyally.

(6) The story ‘The Tempest’ has a happy ending.
(a) Prospero forgives his enemies and regains his Dukedom.
(b) Miranda and Ferdinand fall in love and plan to marry, bringing joy and unity.

o By Air: Nearest airport is Aurangabad.

• Climate: Pleasant in winter; hot in summer; monsoon brings greenery.
